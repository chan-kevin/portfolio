About Me

Hi there! My name is Kevin Chan, and I am a software developer with a background in finance. After spending several years in the finance industry, I decided to make a change and pursue my passion for coding. I am now on a mission to become a Fullstack Software Engineer, specializing in JavaScript, CSS3, HTML5, React / Redux, and Ruby on Rails. As I continue to learn and develop my skills in programming, I have come to appreciate the endless possibilities and challenges that the field offers. The tech industry is constantly evolving and requires continuous learning, and that is something that excites me. I enjoy keeping up with new trends and technologies, and I am always looking for new ways to improve my skills. Throughout my journey as a software developer, I have discovered that I have a passion for creating new things from scratch. Building applications from the ground up has been a fulfilling experience, and I am always eager to try out new ideas and explore different possibilities. Though not all my ideas have been successful, I have learned a lot from my failures and use those experiences to push myself to become a better developer. In my free time, I enjoy tinkering with new technologies, experimenting with new coding languages, and exploring new open-source projects. I am excited about the endless opportunities available in the tech industry and am eager to continue to grow and develop my skills as a software developer. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me, and I look forward to connecting with you soon!
